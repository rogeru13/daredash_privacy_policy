DareDash Privacy Policy

Last Updated: March 19, 2024

Introduction

This Privacy Policy describes how DareDash ("we," "our," or "us") collects, uses, and shares your personal information when you use our iOS application (the "App"). We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring you have a positive experience on our App.

Information We Collect

Information You Provide

  • Account information (email address, username)
  • Profile information (display name, profile picture)
  • User-generated content (dare memories, photos, videos)
  • Communication preferences
  • Phone number (optional, for friend connections)

Information Collected Automatically

  • Device information (device type, iOS version)
  • Usage data (app interactions, feature usage)
  • Performance data (crash reports)
  • IP address for security purposes

Information from Third Parties

  • Contacts information (with your explicit permission)
  • Authentication information from Apple Sign In
  • Photos and videos from your device (with your permission)

How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information to:

  • Provide and maintain the DareDash service
  • Process your dare challenges and memories
  • Manage your friend connections
  • Send you notifications about daily dares
  • Improve and personalize your experience
  • Ensure the security of your account
  • Handle your support requests

Data Storage and Security

Data Storage

  • User data is stored securely using Supabase's encrypted database
  • Media files (photos and videos) are stored in secure cloud storage
  • Local device storage is used for caching and offline access
  • Contacts are cached locally with your permission

Security Measures

  • End-to-end encryption for sensitive data
  • Secure data transmission using TLS/SSL
  • Regular security audits
  • Access controls and authentication
  • Secure password requirements

Data Sharing and Disclosure

We may share your information with:

  • Other users (only the information you choose to share)
  • Service providers who assist in operating our App
  • Legal authorities when required by law

We do not sell your personal information to third parties.

Your Rights and Choices

You have the right to:

  • Access your personal information
  • Update your profile information
  • Delete your account and associated data
  • Control notification preferences
  • Export your data
  • Withdraw consent for contacts access

Specific Features and Data Collection

Contacts Access

  • We only access your contacts with your explicit permission
  • Contact information is used solely for friend connections
  • You can revoke contacts access at any time
  • Contacts are cached locally for 24 hours

Media Access

  • We access your photos and videos only with your permission
  • Media is used for creating and sharing dare memories
  • You can revoke media access at any time
  • Media is stored securely in the cloud

Push Notifications

  • We send push notifications for daily dares
  • You can control notification preferences in the app settings
  • Notifications can be disabled at any time

Friend Connections

  • Friend connections are managed through your contacts
  • You can block users at any time
  • Friend requests require mutual consent

Account Deletion

When you delete your account:

  • All your personal information is removed
  • Your dare memories and media are deleted
  • Your friend connections are removed
  • Your data cannot be recovered

Third-Party Services

Our App uses the following third-party services:

  • Supabase for data storage and authentication
  • Apple's services for authentication and notifications
  • Cloud storage for media files
