Introduction

This Privacy Policy describes how DareDash ("we," "our," or "us") collects, uses, and shares your personal information when you use our iOS application (the "App"). We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring you have a positive experience on our App.

Information We Collect

Information You Provide

  • Account information (email address, username)
  • Profile information (name, profile picture)
  • User-generated content
  • Communication preferences

Information Collected Automatically

  • Device information (device type, operating system version)
  • Usage data (app interactions, feature usage)
  • Performance data (crash reports, app performance metrics)
  • IP address and location data (if applicable)

Information from Third Parties

  • Authentication information from Apple Sign In
  • Analytics data from approved third-party services

How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information to:

  • Provide, maintain, and improve our App
  • Process your transactions
  • Send you technical notices and support messages
  • Respond to your comments and questions
  • Monitor and analyze trends and usage
  • Detect, prevent, and address technical issues
  • Personalize your experience

Data Storage and Security

Data Storage

  • User data is stored securely using industry-standard encryption
  • We use Apple's secure storage solutions (Keychain, UserDefaults)
  • Data is stored in compliance with applicable data protection laws

Security Measures

  • End-to-end encryption for sensitive data
  • Regular security audits and updates
  • Access controls and authentication
  • Secure data transmission using TLS/SSL
